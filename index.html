How it works

01

Drop your footage in

Drag files anywhere in the editor. Video, audio, stills. They are read straight off your disk and cached in the browser, never sent anywhere.

02

03

Pick a resolution and bitrate, hit export, and the file lands in your downloads. No queue, no credits, no watermark.

FAQ

Questions worth answering

Does my video get uploaded anywhere?

No. Files are read locally and cached in your browser's own storage. Exporting writes a file to your downloads folder. Nothing is sent to a server.

What formats can I import?

Whatever your browser can decode, which usually means MP4 and MOV with H.264, WebM, MP3, WAV, M4A, PNG, JPG and WebP. Chrome and Edge decode the widest range.

Why does exporting take as long as the video?

Export records the live timeline through the browser's media recorder, so a two minute sequence takes two minutes. Faster than real time rendering needs WebCodecs, which is on the roadmap.

Will I lose my work if I close the tab?

The editor saves continuously, on a timer and again when the tab closes. Reopen the project from your dashboard and it comes back with media attached.

Is there a watermark?

No, on any tier.

Does it work on a phone?

The site and dashboard do. The editor needs a keyboard and pointer, so it asks you to switch to a desktop.

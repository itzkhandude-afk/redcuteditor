Billing questions

Before you pay anyone

Do I need a paid plan to export 4K?

No. Export runs on your own hardware, so it stays free at every resolution with no watermark.

What happens to my projects if I downgrade?

They stay editable. Cloud copies beyond the free allowance become read only until you free up space or upgrade again.

Can I cancel any time?

Yes, and the plan runs to the end of the period you paid for.

Do you have an affiliate programme?

Planned at 25% recurring for twelve months. The dashboard for it is on the roadmap.

Student and non profit pricing?

50% off Pro on request with proof of status.
